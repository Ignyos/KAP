# Kitchen & Pantry

Kitchen & Pantry helps you organize grocery lists, pantry and fridge checklists, and recipes in one place.

Use the app online:

- https://kap.ignyos.com

## What You Can Do

- Create and manage grocery lists.
- Keep pantry and fridge checklists you can reuse.
- Create recipes and maintain multiple versions of each recipe.
- Manage units of measure from a dedicated Units of Measure page.

## Getting Started

1. Open https://kap.ignyos.com.
2. On the Home screen, choose Grocery Lists, Pantry & Fridge, or Recipes.
3. Add your records and update them as needed.
4. Optional: open the menu and choose Install App to add Kitchen & Pantry to your device.

## How It Works

- Your information is saved in your browser on your device.
- Recipes support version history so you can create and keep separate recipe versions.
